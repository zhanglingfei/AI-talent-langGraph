Real-world Example: 15-page Resume

  • Total Size: ~20,000 tokens (too large for single embed)
  • Strategy: Parent-child chunking with overlap
  • Parent Chunk: 2-page executive summary (2000 tokens)
  • Child Chunks: 5 x 4000 tokens (each work experience)
  • Overlap: 500 tokens between chunks for context
  • Result: 6 embeddings covering full document

Retrieval Strategy

  • Step 1: Search parent chunks for high-level match
  • Step 2: Search child chunks for detailed requirements
  • Step 3: Combine scores with weights (parent: 0.3, child: 0.7)
  • Step 4: Return top chunks with surrounding context
  • Benefit: Captures both overview and specific details

Advanced Chunking Strategies

  • Recursive: RecursiveCharacterTextSplitter(6000, overlap=500)
  • Hierarchical: Doc→Section→Paragraph embeddings
  • Parent-Child: Store both large & small chunks
  • Sliding Window: 6000 chars window, 2000 overlap
  • Hybrid: Summary embedding + detail chunks
